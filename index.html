Our Mission

The Seattle Murder Society is a community of crow enthusiasts dedicated to promoting the well-being of local crows. We organize initiatives that enhance their habitats, educate the public about their ecological importance, and foster a harmonious relationship between crows and the people of Seattle.

Fostering Relationships

Learn how to ethically and responsibly feed crows to befriend them.

Conservation Efforts

Funds raised will be put towards crow rehabilitation.

Research & Education

We collaborate with experts to study crow behavior and share knowledge.
